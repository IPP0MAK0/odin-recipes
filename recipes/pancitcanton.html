Pancit Canton

Description

Pancit Canton is a popular Filipino noodle stir-fry dish often served during birthdays and special occasions. It’s a flavorful mix of noodles, vegetables, and meats.

This dish symbolizes long life in Filipino culture and is a staple at celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 250g pancit canton (egg noodles)
  • 200g chicken or pork, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cabbage, shredded
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add sliced chicken or pork, cook until browned.
  3. Add carrots, cabbage, and bell pepper, stir-fry for 3–5 minutes.
  4. Pour in chicken broth, soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Bring to a boil.
  5. Add pancit canton noodles, mix well, and cook until tender and liquid is absorbed.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
