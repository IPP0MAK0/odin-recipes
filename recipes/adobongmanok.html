Adobong Manok


Description

Adobong Manok, or Chicken Adobo, is one of the most beloved Filipino dishes. It’s a savory, tangy, and slightly sweet stew made with chicken simmered in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices.

This comfort food is often paired with steamed rice and is perfect for both family meals and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and bay leaves for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pot, sauté onions and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Pour in the marinade, add peppercorns, and simmer for 30–40 minutes until chicken is tender.
  5. Adjust seasoning with sugar if desired. Serve hot with rice.
